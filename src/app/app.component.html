Shisen-Sho - A Japanese tile game

Sometimes referred to as 'Shisen', 'Four Rivers' or simply 'Rivers,' Shisen-sho is a Japanese tile-based game similar to Mahjong solitaire.

How to play Shisen-sho?

It's a single player game, and a its core, a game of match-two. The goal of the game is to clear the board by removing tiles two by two.
However, the challenge is to match them according a certain path, as this simple rule defines it:

Two pieces can be removed only if they can be connected with a maximum of three lines.
One line - Tiles can be removed.
Two lines - Tiles can be removed.
Three lines - Tiles can be removed.
Four lines - Tiles can not be removed.
